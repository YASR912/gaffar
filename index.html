Welcome to the Tribute Website

This website is dedicated to Field Marshal Gaafar Muhammad an-Nimeiry, who served as the Chairman of the National Revolutionary Command Council and was a prominent leader in Sudanese history.

Biography

Gaafar Muhammad an-Nimeiry was born on January 1, 1930, in Sudan. He served as the Chairman of the National Revolutionary Command Council from 1971 until his removal in 1985.

During his time in power, Nimeiry enacted several political, economic, and social reforms. He was known for his leadership during a period of great change in Sudanese history.

Leadership & Political Influence

As Chairman of the National Revolutionary Command Council, Nimeiry oversaw significant changes in Sudan, including the nationalization of key industries, major economic reforms, and his controversial policies toward religion and politics.

His leadership transformed Sudan but also faced criticism for his authoritarian style and repression of opposition parties.
